Skull Soup

Skull Soup is a game that was created as a class and then built upon by each student as we parted ways to customize our versions.
My version includes plenty particle effects and an internal system that generates a random order that is required to follow while collecting the falling objects. It also includes a method to remove any incorrect objects caught and a special object that multiplies points gained for a short durration.
